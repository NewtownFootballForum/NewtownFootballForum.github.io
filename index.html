About Us

Located in the heart of Newtown, Kolkata, the Newtown Football Forum is a vibrant and inclusive community dedicated to celebrating the spirit of football. Whether you're a passionate player, a devoted fan, or someone just beginning to explore the beautiful game, this is your home.

Our mission is simple — to promote football at the grassroots level, foster talent, and create a platform where people of all ages can come together through the love of the sport. We host local tournaments, skill development sessions, match screenings, and regular community events that bring the football fraternity of Newtown and even different parts from Kolkata closer than ever.

Join us to stay updated on local football news, upcoming matches, training schedules, and more. At Newtown Football Forum, we believe football is more than a game — it's a way to build community, inspire the next generation, and share unforgettable moments.

Come be a part of Newtown’s growing football movement. Let’s play, cheer, and grow together.
